Why Get Vaccinated?

Getting vaccinated is the best way to protect yourself and others from the flu. It reduces the risk of severe illness, hospitalization, and even death.

Where to Get Vaccinated?

Find a vaccination center near you. Visit your local health department or check with your healthcare provider for flu vaccine availability.
